How it began

From a Backyard Garden
to Your Doorstep.

SSD Nursery was founded in 2018 by a family of passionate gardeners in Hyderabad, Telangana. What started as a weekend hobby — propagating rare tropical plants and sharing them with neighbours — quickly grew into something much larger.

We noticed that while people loved the idea of indoor plants, they struggled to find healthy, well-cared-for plants from trusted sources. Nurseries were inconsistent, care guidance was absent, and delivery often arrived in poor condition.

So we built the nursery we always wanted to buy from. Every plant is grown in our climate-controlled greenhouses, inspected by our botanists, and shipped in custom packaging designed to keep them thriving in transit.

What we stand for

Our Core Values

Every decision we make comes back to these three principles.

Plant Wellbeing First

Every plant is grown with patience and care. We never rush growth or cut corners on soil, water or light — because a healthy plant is our only product.

Sustainability Always

We use biodegradable packaging, rainwater harvesting in our greenhouses, and source only ethically propagated specimens — never wild-collected.

Customer Trust

Our 7-day healthy plant guarantee isn't just a policy — it's a promise. If your plant arrives unhealthy, we replace it, no questions asked.
